About Resume Optimizer

Resume Optimizer is a web application that analyzes your resume against a provided job description using advanced NLP techniques to provide an ATS (Applicant Tracking System) compatibility score and actionable feedback.

How It Works

  • Upload your resume (PDF/DOCX) and paste the job description
  • Our system processes your resume and extracts key information including:
    • Skills and technologies using SkillNER
    • Work experience and education history
    • Formatting and structure analysis
  • Advanced ATS scoring engine calculates a comprehensive score based on:
    • Skill matching with the job description
    • Keyword relevance (TF-IDF with n-grams)
    • Formatting and ATS-friendly structure
    • Content organization and readability

Key Features

  • Skill Analysis: Identifies and categorizes your skills
  • Missing Skills: Highlights skills from the job description that are missing from your resume
  • Formatting Check: Ensures your resume is ATS-friendly
  • Actionable Feedback: Provides specific suggestions for improvement

Why This Matters

Over 75% of resumes are rejected by ATS before they're even seen by a human. This tool helps you optimize your resume to pass through ATS filters and get noticed by recruiters.
